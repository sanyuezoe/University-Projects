In the love-themed issue, we embark on a captivating exploration of love in its various forms, where we encounter the enchanting stories of three extraordinary characters: Clytemnestra, Circe, and the book "Girl Meets Boy." Through the skillful storytelling of our talented writers, we unravel the intricacies of their relationships, their personal journeys, and the profound impact of love on their lives.

we invite you to immerse yourself in these compelling narratives of love's triumphs and challenges. Prepare to be captivated by the multifaceted nature of love and the transformative power it holds in the lives of these extraordinary characters.
