Hidden

Domestic Violence in Italy

You are walking around in a City in Italy. It is the year 2021 and we are still experiencing the pandemic. However, the streets are full again. You meet a lot of people on your way and feel a bit overwhelmed by that.


While continuing walking you face the floor and concentrate on your own steps. You have just read about rising cases of domestic violence during the pandemic and are wondering why this is such a hidden topic. You can not understand how this can happen, again and again and almost remain unspoken in the public talk.


Suddenly you stop. A small object catches your attention. There, in the corner of the street, almost hidden but you recognize it. Against the grey concrete it is glowing. You pick up the object!
